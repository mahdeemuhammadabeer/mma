SANDA

A modern Chinese martial art and combat sport that combines elements of traditional martial arts with full-contact techniques. It involves punching, kicking, throwing, and wrestling, making it similar to kickboxing. Developed by Chinese military for self-defense and close combat, Sanda emphasizes practical combat skills and is practiced both as a sport and a self-defense system..

MMA

It’s a full-contact combat sport that combines techniques from different fighting styles like: Boxing (punching), Muay Thai (kicks, elbows, knees), Wrestling (takedowns, control), Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (submissions, ground fighting), Judo (throws, clinch work). The goal is to defeat your opponent by: Knockout (KO), Technical knockout (TKO), Submission (tap out), Judges’ decision.
